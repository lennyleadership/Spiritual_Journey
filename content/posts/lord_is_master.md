詩篇 127:1 CUNP-神  
若不是耶和華建造房屋， 建造的人就枉然勞力；   
若不是耶和華看守城池， 看守的人就枉然警醒。  

‪Psalms‬ 127:1 NLT 
Unless the Lord builds a house, the work of the builders is wasted.   
Unless the Lord protects a city, guarding it with sentries will do no good.
